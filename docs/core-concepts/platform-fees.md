# Platform Fees

YieldPlay charges 2-5% platform fees on the yield generated.

## Fee Structure

| Plan | Fee | Best For |
|------|-----|----------|
| Free Tier | 5% | Testing, small games |
| Growth | 3% | Mid-size games |
| Enterprise | 2% | Large studios |

## What Fees Cover

- DeFi yield farming
- Smart contract execution
- VRF randomness
- Infrastructure
- Support

## No Hidden Fees

- ✅ No monthly subscription
- ✅ No per-transaction fees
- ✅ No withdrawal fees
- ✅ No minimum volume

You only pay when you earn.
